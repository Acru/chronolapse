Chronolapse

Easily create your own timelapse videos.

Welcome toChronolapse

Chronolapse takes screenshots and webcam captures on a schedule then processes and combines them into a timelapse video ready to share.

tl;dr

Chronolapse includes everything you need to automatically capture still images from your desktop or webcam, process them a bit (rotate, scale, PiP), then combine them into complete timelapse videos.

Example Timelapses

Here are some example timelapse videos made by the community

Chronolapse includes all the tools you need to create a timelapse video without locking you in to any particular software.

Screenshots and Webcams

Capture screenshots from one or multiple monitors and still images from webcams.

Postprocess captured images

Crop, Scale, and create Picture-in-Picture Effects.

Encode Video

Create video from your still images with custom frame rate.

Add Audio

Easily add audio to completed videos.

  • About

Chronolapse was created byCollin Greenand released as free and open source software for anybody to use. Chronolapse was originally created to help create timelapse videos covering hackathons (specifically ludum dare). It has since helped people create everything from stop motion videos to timelapses of medical research, painting, 3D modeling, gameplay, and engine rebuilds. Use your imagination!
